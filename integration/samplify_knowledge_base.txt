PRODUCT INFORMATION:
Samplify is an environmental sampling solution that provides comprehensive water and air quality testing. Our platform offers real-time data entry, GPS capabilities, and detailed reporting features.

SUPPORTED SAMPLE TYPES:
1. Water/Effluent Testing
2. Ambient Air/Fugitive Emissions
3. Stack Emission Monitoring
4. Environmental Sampling
5. Air/Swabs Analysis
6. Food Safety Testing
7. Noise Level Assessment
8. Solid Waste Analysis

MOBILE APP FEATURES:
- Real-time data entry with offline capabilities
- GPS location tracking for sample collection
- Photo documentation with geotagging
- Instant report generation
- Compliance tracking with regulatory standards
- Multi-user collaboration tools
- Cloud synchronization across devices

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS:
Q: Can I use Samplify for soil testing?
A: While our primary focus is water and air quality, our Professional and Enterprise plans include soil testing modules.

Q: Is there a desktop version available?
A: Yes, we offer a web-based dashboard that complements our mobile app. All data syncs seamlessly between platforms.

Q: How quickly can I get set up with Samplify?
A: Most users are fully operational within 24 hours of signing up. We provide onboarding assistance to help you configure the system for your specific needs.

Q: Do you offer custom integrations?
A: Yes, our Enterprise plan includes custom API integrations with existing laboratory information management systems (LIMS) and enterprise resource planning (ERP) software.
